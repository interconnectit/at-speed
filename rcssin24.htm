Stage Information

Stage 24 - Penmachno South

12:13 | £5 per car

Stage length: 8.32 miles

Principal marshalling clubs: Wallasey MC, Stockport MC

Location: 3 miles south of A5 at Conwy Falls (near Betws-y-Coed).

Car parks: Car park A, south of Penmachno giving access to start and mid point hairpin.

Fact file: length of stage in 1995 - 8.36 miles. Fastest time 7 mins 33 secs (McRae).

Car parks open: 07:00 Monday

A mixture of very fast roads, steep hills, tight hairpins and fearsome drops.

Routes: from the south, via Ffestiniog, B4391, B4407 and north on unclassified road to car park A. This route is also used by competitors and will be closed to spectators 30 minutes prior to the first car. From the north, via A5 and B4406 to car park A.

Warning: Parking at the roadside or on verges is strictly forbidden - the rally will be stopped if the school bus can not get through - offenders will be towed away and charged for the privilege.
